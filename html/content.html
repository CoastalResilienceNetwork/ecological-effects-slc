Type of Flooding

Frequent flooding is expected to occur daily to every other day. Semi-frequent flooding is expected to occur on a weekly to monthly basis. Infrequent flooding might occur one to several times a year. Rare flooding are extreme events that occur once in multiple decades.

Scenario Year

Excluding the Recent scenario, each of the scenarios reflect an increase in regional sea level rise linked to projected global increases in mean sea level around the respective time period based on the Fifth Assessment Report of the Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (2013).
